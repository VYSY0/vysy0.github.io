HiraethSMP [ON-GOING]

HiraethSMP is an RP/Economic/Nation Realm focused on a 16+ community, but prioritizes those who are above 18.

It encourages nationwide cooperation via events to make the gameplay more engaging.

The lore in this Realm is fully written by created characters, but character creation is not mandatory. If you're a PvP or PvE player, there's an option to toggle it in Addon Settings.

First time hearing about it? Join via the panel on the left! Without it, joining is not possible.

Races & More [ON-GOING]

HSMP's Races & More is an Minecraft: Bedrock Edition addon which adds more than it should
Primary focus of the addon is to add Races, Weapons and customization to the created characters, but it added much more.

The addon offers it's own easy to navigate menu which allows players to control their game via one-click command.
Players also are able to view their information in this menu.

It also adds unique races with their own decorations! Which also means, helmets are disabled and no one can use them.
Although helmets are disabled, all players are getting netherite-level protection in helmet slot to avoid problems.
