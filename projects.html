HiraethSMP

HiraethSMP is an RP/Economic/Nation Realm focused on a 16+ community, but prioritizes those who are above 18.

It encourages nationwide cooperation via events to make the gameplay more engaging.

The lore in this Realm is fully written by created characters, but character creation is not mandatory. If you're a PvP or PvE player, there's an option to toggle it in Addon Settings.

First time hearing about it? Join via the panel on the left! Without it, joining is not possible.
