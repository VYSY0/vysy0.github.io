Hello!

I'm Vys, an MC:BE Addon developer, HSMP Owner, and a passionate Website developer! I love creating interactive experiences, whether it's through games, web projects, or collaborative communities.


My projects include HiraethSMP, this website, and a planned 2D MMORPG Game developed in the Open Source Godot Engine. Each project is a labor of love, blending creativity, functionality, and community engagement.


All of my projects are Open Source and published on GitHub. I believe in transparency and collaboration, allowing my community — and anyone interested — to explore, learn, and contribute. Sharing knowledge is one of the core principles that drives me as a developer.


Aside from coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, optimizing workflows, and connecting with other developers. Whether it's designing custom game mechanics, building intuitive web interfaces, or experimenting with new frameworks, I’m always eager to learn and improve.


I'm always striving to do my best, and all of my projects are funded out of my own pocket. Even though I work a full-time job, I always make time for my passion: programming. I enjoy creating with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GodotScript, experimenting with new ideas, and constantly improving my skills. For me, coding isn’t just a hobby—it’s a way to challenge myself, learn continuously, and bring my creative visions to life.

Balancing work and personal projects can be challenging, but I find it incredibly rewarding to see my projects grow and evolve. Every line of code is an opportunity to learn something new, and sharing my work with the community makes the journey even more fulfilling.


If you enjoy my projects and want to support my work, you can do so via Ko-Fi. Contributions go directly to HiraethSMP and my addon projects. I don't rely on my projects for rent or living expenses—everything I create is for others and for myself, to share my perspective, life experiences, and lessons I've learned along the way.


If you're curious about my work, want to contribute, or just want to say hi, feel free to reach out! I love seeing new ideas come to life and helping others grow in the process.

About

HiraethSMP is a community-driven Minecraft Bedrock Edition server that aims to provide modded-like and server-like experiences in a welcoming environment. Adding custom mechanics, features, and QOL improvements.


HSMP adds features like: Races, Economy, In-Game currency, Custom Mobs, Custom Map with possible OOB exploring.
