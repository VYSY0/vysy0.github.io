HiraethSMP

HiraethSMP is a community-driven Minecraft Bedrock Edition server that blends modded-style features with traditional server gameplay, creating a welcoming and engaging environment for players. The server introduces unique mechanics, custom features, and QOL improvements that enhance daily gameplay and player interactions.

HSMP offers a wide range of exciting features, including: Races – diverse character classes such as Humans, Dwarfs, Elves, Therianthropes, and Tanarri, allowing for varied playstyles and roles within the community, Economy – a trading system with custom merchants offering resources, blocks, and region-based items, In-Game Currency – HiraDollar, a points-based currency that cannot be purchased with real money, supporting player trade and interaction, Custom Mobs – both hostile and neutral creatures that add depth, challenge, and life to the world, Custom Map – initially 5k x 5k blocks, soon expanding to 10k x 10k with new regions, allowing for OOB exploration beyond the normal boundaries.

The main goal of HSMP is to foster a friendly and inclusive community where players can create lasting memories, enjoy their time in-game, and actively engage with one another. The server emphasizes player interaction, character progression, and the exploration of a rich, dynamic world.
